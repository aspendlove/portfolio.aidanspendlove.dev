Switch Media Downloader

About this piece

Switch Media Downloader is a tool that I have only recently created. It came to be when I found it difficult to share videos from my Nintendo Switch to my friend through messaging apps on my phone. I leveraged the fact that I have my own custom server to make a creative solution.

Nintendo Switches have a couple ways of getting screenshots and videos off of the console: posting to social media, or sending the files one by one over the local network. The latter is too slow and cumbersome, so I looked if I could do anything with the social media route. I figured that I could create a fake twitter account, connect it to my switch, and then run a service on my server to watch that Twitter feed for posts, which is exactly what I did. Creating and connecting the account to my switch was the easy part, but I needed to create a custom service to continually watch the Twitter feed. I had to create a Twitter developer account, and with some trial and error, I was able to pull together multiple projects to create one utility that can automatically download screenshots and videos as soon as they are posted to a Twitter feed. I can now very easily post a video to twitter from my switch, and within a minute it will have shown up on my phone ready to send.
