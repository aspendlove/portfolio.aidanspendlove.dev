Spreadsheet Wars

About this piece

Spreadsheet wars is an assignment from my ARTX 2000, Computers and the Arts, class at the University of Utah. I stitched together different videos from archive.org to create a form of video collage. While browsing archive.org, I found some old "Computer Chronicles" videos that discussed many hot computer topics from 1983-2002. One that was particularly interesting, was titled "Spreadsheet Wars, Lotus123 vs the World". I thought this was a silly comparison, so I took some footage from a first-person shooter game, Team Fortress 2, and created a more literal war of the spreadsheets.
