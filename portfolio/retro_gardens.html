About this piece

This is a Photoshop collage I created in my ARTX 2000 - Computers and the Arts class at the University of Utah. My vision was an uncanny mashup of video games and nature. I wanted to imagine a world where their two worlds collided; where ladybugs and tiny video game soldiers could coexist.
