Celeste Trailer

About this piece

An assignment in my Art 3600 - Writing for New Media class, tasked me with creating a project made purely out of appropriated content. For my submission I created a trailer for the game Celeste using "found" footage of other people playing the game. The assignment was used to start a discussion around the idea of ownership and originality. Everything is derived from somewhere, and this takes it to the most extreme.
