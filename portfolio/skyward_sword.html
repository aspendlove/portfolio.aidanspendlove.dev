Skyward Sword 3DS Theme

About this piece

This is a custom theme for the Nintendo 3DS based on the game The Legend of Zelda: Skyward Sword. A 3DS Theme dictates many things about the console's operating system, including the background, sound effects, colors, and other textures used by the operating system. My theme uses sound effects from Skyward Sword, official art as the background, background music from the game, and the colors / textures are my own.
