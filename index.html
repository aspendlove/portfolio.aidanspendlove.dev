About Me

I am a computer science major here at the University of Utah, where I am currently studying for a bachelor's degree in computer science. I also have a minor in Art Technology which provides me with a well needed creative outlet.

I love to program in many different languages, listen to music, watch movies/shows, play video games, work with computers, and repair electronic devices.

Currently serving the community by helping people learn and use computers at the FairPark Family Learning Center, I hope to eventually move into a software development role.
